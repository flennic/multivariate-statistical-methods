The Mahalanobis distance is represented as,
\[d^2_M(\vec{x} - \bar{x}) = (\vec{x} - \bar{x})^T \boldsymbol{C}^{-1}(\vec{x} - \bar{x}),\]
where \textbf{C} is the sample covariance matrix calculated from the
data.
